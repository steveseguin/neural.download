The Process

Ninety minutes in a scanner. Years of insight.

The scan is completely non-invasive. No contrast agents, no radiation. You sit in a chair and we capture everything we need. The hard part is what happens after.

1

Scan

A 90-minute session at a partner imaging center near you. We capture resting-state fMRI, task-based fMRI, high-density EEG, and DTI. The scans map both the physical wiring and the electrical activity of your brain in real time.

2

Reconstruct

Our pipeline aligns and fuses all four data modalities into a single connectome — a map of the structural pathways and functional connections that make your cognition, memory, and personality work the way they do. This takes about a week of compute.

3

Model

Machine learning trained on thousands of other neural profiles builds your Cognitive Profile — a living computational model that you can update with future scans. It tracks change over time. It surfaces things worth talking to your doctor about.

Privacy

Your data. Literally no one else's.

Brain scans are the most intimate data that exists. More personal than DNA. We treat them that way.

There is no business model that involves selling your data. There never will be. If that changes, the company has failed.

  • You own your neural data. Full stop. We process it at your direction.
  • Encrypted at rest and in transit. Decryption keys derived from your credentials.
  • Zero third-party access without your explicit, revocable consent.
  • Full export in open formats whenever you want.
  • Permanent deletion on request — including backups.

Designed for HIPAA and GDPR. Pursuing independent security audits. For data rights: privacy@neural.download.
