The Science

How Neural Download works

A three-stage process grounded in established neuroscience and validated clinical imaging protocols.

1

Acquisition

A 90-minute session at one of our partner imaging centers. We capture resting-state and task-based fMRI, high-density EEG, and DTI scans. Completely non-invasive — no contrast agents, no radiation.

2

Reconstruction

Our pipeline aligns and fuses multi-modal data to build a personalized connectome — a detailed map of the structural and functional pathways that define your cognition, memory, and personality.

3

Modeling & Insight

Machine learning models trained on thousands of neural profiles generate your Cognitive Profile — a living, updateable model that surfaces actionable medical insights and tracks cognitive change over time.

About

Our mission

Neural Download was founded in 2024 by a team of computational neuroscientists, clinical neuroimaging researchers, and machine learning engineers who shared a conviction: the tools to understand the human brain at the individual level already exist — they just have not been made accessible.

We are building the infrastructure to give every person a detailed, actionable map of their own mind. Not decades from now, but today, using validated imaging techniques and modern computational methods.

Our Series A round of $78M, led by investors at the intersection of biotech and frontier AI, allows us to scale our imaging partnerships, expand our modeling pipeline, and open our first consumer-facing neural mapping centers in 2027.

We are headquartered in San Francisco, with research operations in Boston and Zurich.

Privacy

Your data, your control

Neural data is among the most sensitive personal information that exists. We take this responsibility seriously. Our privacy framework is designed to exceed current regulatory requirements and set a new standard for neural data stewardship.

Core principles

  • Ownership: You own your neural data. We process it on your behalf and at your direction.
  • Encryption: All neural profiles are encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Decryption keys are derived from your credentials and are never stored by us.
  • No third-party access: Your data is never sold, shared, or made accessible to third parties without your explicit, informed, revocable consent.
  • Full portability: Export your complete neural profile in open, documented formats at any time.
  • Right to deletion: Request permanent deletion of all your data, including backups, at any time. Deletion is completed within 30 days.

Compliance

Neural Download is HIPAA-compliant and adheres to GDPR requirements for all users. We undergo annual third-party security audits and maintain SOC 2 Type II certification.

For our full privacy policy or to exercise your data rights, contact privacy@neural.download.
