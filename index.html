Our Technology

Quantum Neural Scanning

Our revolutionary scanning technology utilizes multiple cesium gas arrays positioned around the head, each quantum-pumped and excited by precision lasers. Neural activity causes measurable changes in the transmission of polarized light through the cesium, allowing for detailed 3D mapping of brain activity.

AI-Driven Analysis

By combining quantum scanning data with our advanced AI models, we create comprehensive neural activity maps that maintain high accuracy while reducing scanning time and computational requirements. This hybrid approach allows for rapid processing and real-time analysis.
